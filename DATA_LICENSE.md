# Data Licensing and Attribution

This repository separates code licensing from data licensing.

- Code: see `LICENSE` (`Apache-2.0`)
- Final aligned corpus in `data/<lang>/aligned/`: `CC BY-SA 4.0`

The aligned corpus is derived from Wikipedia and Vikidia. Reuse of the corpus must preserve attribution to the upstream sources and comply with the applicable share-alike requirements.

The upstream terms for source text remain relevant for the raw and lightly transformed materials stored under:

- `data/<lang>/raw/`
- `data/<lang>/wiki/`
- `data/<lang>/viki/`

## Upstream Sources

### Wikipedia

- Site: https://www.wikipedia.org/
- Licensing and reuse guidance: https://foundation.wikimedia.org/wiki/Policy:Terms_of_Use

### Vikidia

- Site: https://www.vikidia.org/
- Copyright and licensing information: https://en.vikidia.org/wiki/Vikidia:Copyright

## Attribution Requirements

When redistributing or reusing the aligned corpus, include:

- a citation to this repository and the associated paper
- a notice that the corpus is derived from Wikipedia and Vikidia
- links to the relevant upstream licensing pages
- a note that the data consist of aligned and processed sentence pairs rather than raw source dumps

Suggested short attribution statement:

> This corpus contains aligned and processed text derived from Wikipedia and Vikidia. Please refer to the upstream licensing and attribution pages linked in this repository.

This file summarizes the repository policy for the released aligned corpus and does not replace the original upstream license terms.
