Sony
Sony, (ソニー株式会社Sonī Kabushiki Gaisha in Japanese), is a company headquartered in Tokyo, Japan.
One of the largest and important companies worldwide in the industry of audio and video
The company was founded in 1946 by Masaru Ibukta and Akio Morita, administrated by Kenichiro Yoshida.
during the 80's and the 90's, Sony was the leader of all the industries of technology section worldwide.
It is known for creating the Compact Disc, the "Walkman", the Handycam and the famous PlayStation.
