Republic
The red countries are monarchies, while the blue are with other governments
Not all republics are democracies.
Countries like Myanmar, Turkmenistan and in the past Chile weren't democracies.
Most countries became republics after they abolished the monarchy (like Italy, Greece and Nepal) or became independent (like most American, African and Asian countries).
A few countries abolished the republic and became monarchies later on.
An example is the Roman Republic, which later on became the Roman Empire.
The Netherlands were a republic after the independence from Spain, but are since 1815 a kingdom.
