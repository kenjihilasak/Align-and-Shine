Albatross
The albatross is the biggest bird in the marine world.
Its wings are white with a light orange beak.
The female albatross makes eggs.
The albatross is called a sea bird.
The Albatross flies.
The scientific name of Albatross is Diomedeide.
The Albatross eat fish and krill.
The Albatross live in couples.
ALBATROSS OF TRISTAN
The descend of Albatross is Albatross of Tristan.
