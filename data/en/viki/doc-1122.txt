Benjamin Netanyahu in 2012
Benjamin "Bibi" Netanyahu (21 October 1949, Tel Aviv) is an Israeli politician who heads the rightwing Likud party.
He served as Prime Minister a total of 15 years, the longest of any in that position.
Netanyahu is the first Israeli prime minister to be born in Israel after the establishment of the State, his predecessors being immigrants.
He was raised in a secularJewish family.
