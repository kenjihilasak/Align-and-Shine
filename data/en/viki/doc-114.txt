Eoin Colfer (14 May 1965, Wexford, Ireland).
He is a famous writer who created Iron Man and Artemis Fowl.
These books are widely known in the world.
Eoin Colfer was born in Wexford on the South-East of Ireland.
Eoin's father was an a elementary school teacher, an historian and an artist of note and Eoin's mother was a drama teacher.
He studied in Dublin, returning to Wexford to teach primary school children .
His first book Benny and Omar was published in 1998.
The first Artemis Fowl book was published and became a New York Times best seller in October 2009.
He married in 1991.
He lives in Ireland with is wife and two children's.
In September 2008, Colfer was commissioned to write the sixth installment of the Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy series.
In October 2016, in a contract with Marvel Comics, he realised Iron man: the Gauntlet
