Klix.ba
Klix.ba is one of the most visited web portal and Web-site in Bosnia and Herzegovina.
It was created in 2000 as , and changed its name in 2012.
Every day it brings information and news from Bosnia and Herzegovina and the world.
The founder and owner is the web development company from Sarajevo InterSoft d.o.o.
Sarajevo, has owned by Dario Šimić and Mario Šimić (brothers).
The portal was created in October 2000 with name name , and in April 2012 it changed name to Klix.ba with the aim of content and regional development.
At the beginning, mostly news from the world was published, with entertaining content, without black chronicles and without politics.
This forum was quickly introduced, which has remained now like the most visited internet forum in Bosnia and Herzegovina.
According to Google Analytics data for June 2017, only the desktop and mobile version of the Klix.ba portal was visited by 2.7 million users who achieved 50 million page loads.
In addition, the Klix.ba application for Android and iOS is used by more than 110,000 users.
