The Electromagnetic spectrum is a big name for types of radiation.
The types are radio waves, microwaves, infrared, visible light, ultraviolet, x-rays, and gamma rays.
Radio waves are the lowest energy waves.
They are huge, they are as tall as buildings.
They are used for radio and TV signals.
Microwaves are mainly used for microwave ovens.
Radar uses microwaves, not radio waves.
They are much smaller than a radio wave.
Infrared rays are shorter than microwaves and used for thermographs.
We can feel infrared sometimes.
It is used for electrical heaters also.
