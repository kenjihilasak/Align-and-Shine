Rhode Island
Rhode Island (officially State of Rhode Island and Providence Plantations) is one of the 50 states of United States and is located in the New England region.
The capital of the state is Providence, the population is of 1,050,292 habitants (2012) and the area is of 4,002 km² being the smallest U.S. state.
