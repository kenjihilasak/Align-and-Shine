Until 1990, as Estonian SSR, Estonia was a republic of the Soviet Union.
On 30 March 1990, the leaders of Estonia declared that the control of their country by the Soviet Union from 1940 was illegal and declared independence of Estonia.
On 6 September 1991, the Soviet Union recognized the Estonia's independence.
