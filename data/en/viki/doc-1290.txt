Parrot
A parrot is a bird.
It has a broad beak, multicolor wings and feathers and a long feathered tail.
Parrots are very colorful animals.
He weighs between 1.2 kg - 12kg.
They have a robust body and short legs.
The position of their body is, most of the time, erected.
They have 4 fingers and are skilled animals in climbing.
