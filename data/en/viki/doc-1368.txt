Petrus Plancius Instructing Students in the Science of Navigation
In 1592 he published his best known world map named "Nova et exacta Terrarum Tabula geographica et hydrographica".
He wrote journals and shipping maps.
He made a new way for finding longitude.
He was the first to use the Mercator projection for navigational maps.
Plancius made the map, Exacta & accurata delinatio… regionibus China, Cauchinchina, Camboja, sive Champa, Syao, Malacca, Arracan & Pegu, that was in Jan Huygen van Linschoten’s popular book, Itinerario (1596).
↑"New and exact geographic and hydrographic map of the world".
