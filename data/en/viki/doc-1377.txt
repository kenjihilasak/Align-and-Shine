Anchisaurus
Drawing of Anchisaurus
The Anchisaurus is one of the smaller sauropods, also a slow walker that lived in (now) USA.
It was discovered in 1885, by his footprints.
Its size was up to 8 feet long, and his weight was up to 77 pounds (35 kg).
It eats ground-level plants.
It had a thumb claw and blunt teeth.
