Club Penguin
Club Penguin's logo
Club Penguin is a children multiplayer online game, which has many mini-games and activities.
It was developed by Disney Channel Entertainment (Disney Canada Inc.).
The penguins are the main characters of the game, they can walk, talk, play mini-games and other activities.
Club Penguin originally started as a chat called Penguin Chat, in 2004, then went Penguin Chat 2 and later Penguin Chat 3, but realized they needed more than just a chat, so that Club Penguin was opened to the public 24 October 2005 by Lane Merrifield.
New Horizon, the initial parent company of Club Penguin, was acquired by Disney for $350 millions.
