Wikia
Wikia is a free wiki hosting site (called a wiki farm).
The site was founded in 2004 by Jimmy Wales and Angela Beesley.
The site was called Wikicities until 2006, when it was changed to Wikia to avoid confusion with actual cities.
According to Wikia, it has over 400,000 wiki communities.
Wikia does not currently offer private wikis.
