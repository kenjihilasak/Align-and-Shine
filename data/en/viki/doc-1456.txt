The Bay of Bengal is the largest bay in the world.
It is an almost triangular bay located in the northern part of the Indian Ocean.
It is bordered by India and Sri Lanka to the west, India and Bangladesh to the north, and Myanmar and Thailand to the east.
The Andaman and Nicobar Islands of India are located right in the middle of the Bay of Bengal.
