South Park
The sitcom talks about 4 kids : Stan Marsh, Kyle Broflovski, Eric Cartman and Kenny McCormick that all lives at South Park, a city from Colorado.
South Park's humor is absurd, parodic, sarcastic, gravelly and scatologic and often talks about sensitive topics such as death or current controversial topics.
South Park often causes controversies because of certain episodes.
