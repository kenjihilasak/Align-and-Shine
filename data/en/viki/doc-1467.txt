Roblox
Roblox is a popular massively multiplayer online video game and game creation system that allows users to design their own games and play a wide variety of different types of games created by other users.
The game was created by David Baszucki and Erik Cassel, and it was made in 2006.
Roblox came to be when David Bazucki and Erik Cassel started working on a piece 2D modeling software.
It was released under the name of Interactive Physics, but they thought that if it was 3D, it would be better.
So in 2003-2004, they started working on Roblox.
In 2004, they released a small beta of Roblox called Dynablocks, and finally, in 2006, Roblox released to the public.
