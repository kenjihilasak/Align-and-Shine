Jurassic
A dinosaur in the Jurassic period.
The Jurassic period is the middle section in the Mesozoic era and saw some incredible and amazing life forms ever to roam the Earth and swim the seas.
The Jurassic period began after the mass extinction that ended the Triassic.
In the Jurassic, the dinosaurs dominated the land fauna but they were not alone.
Early mammals were mostly very small herbivores or insectivores and were not in competition with the larger reptiles.
There was a massive climate change from the Triassic to the Jurassic.
The Jurassic was a lush, warm tropical climate just like a forest which helped the reptiles to thrive in that time.
It also has humid and drippy subtropical feel to it.
