Republic of Bosnia and Herzegovina (Bosnian: Република Босна и Херцеговина/Republika Bosna i Hercegovina) was a state in the Europe existing from 1992 to 1995.
Bosnia and Herzegovina seceded from the disintegrating SFR of Yugoslavia in March 1992.
The war in Bosnia(1992-1994), lasted throughout the existence of the republic.
