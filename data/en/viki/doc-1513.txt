French cuisine
French cuisine is well-known throughout the world.
Every region in France has its specialties.
The concept of gastronomy comes from France.
Marie-Antoine Carême is a historic Parisian chef; he is very famous because he invented a lot of French recipes.
Fernand Point, Paul Bocuse, les frères Troisgros, Bernard Loiseau, and Joël Robuchon are more modern chefs from Paris.
