Turkey
This article is about the country.
For the bird, see Turkey (bird).
Turkey (or Republic of Turkey) is a transcontinental country located in Europe and at the west of Asia.
The country has 76 667 864 inhabitants.
Before, the land was a part of the Byzantine Empire, which lasted until 1453 and also a part of the Seljuq Empire.
Then came the Ottoman Empire that lasted until just after the World War I.
The empire then after the World War I was divided by the occupation forces until the Turkish war of independence 1919-1922.
The country "Republic of Turkey" then has been established right after the traits in Lausanne 1923.
