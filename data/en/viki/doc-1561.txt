Andersen in 1869
Hans Christian Andersen (1805-1875) was a Danish writer.
He was born in poverty in Odense, Denmark to a struggling shoemaker.
He grew up longing for a better life.
At age 14, he went to Copenhagen, Denmark.
He hoped to find a career in the theatre, either as an actor or dancer.
After years of rejections and disappointments, Andersen finally gained fame with a book about a walking tour of Copenhagen.
He then decided to settle upon a career as a writer.
