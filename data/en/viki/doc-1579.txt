Gaetano Donizetti, about 1835
Domenico Gaetano Maria Donizetti was an Italian composer.
He was born on 29 November 1797 in Bergamo, Italy.
He married Virginia Vasselli.
None of their three children survived.
He was insane in his last years, and lived in a hospital.
He died in Bergamo on 8 April 1848.
