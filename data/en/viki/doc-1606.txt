Nijinsky as Petrushka, 1911
Vaslav Nijinsky (1889-1950) was a Russian ballet dancer and choreographer.
Nijinsky was born on 12 March 1889 in Kiev, Russian Empire.
His parents were famous circus performers.
Nijinsky was 9 when he joined the Imperial Ballet School in St Petersburg.
He graduated in 1907, and became a member of the Imperial Ballet.
Nijinsky and his daughter Kyra, 1916
In 1913, he married Romola de Pulszky.
In June 1914, they had a daughter, Kyra.
This marriage caused him to be dismissed from the Ballets Russes.
Nijinsky grew mentally unstable in his later years.
He settled in Switzerland with his family.
He spent time in mental hospitals and asylums.
He never danced again, and died in a clinic in London on April 8, 1950.
He is buried in Montmartre Cemetery in Paris.
