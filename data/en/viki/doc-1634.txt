New York
This article is about the U.S. state.
For the U.S. city, see New York City.
New York is a state in the northeastern United States.
It is the third most populous state, after California and Texas.
In 2015, it had 19,795,791 people.
The state capital is Albany.
New York City, the largest city in New York, is also the largest city in the United States.
