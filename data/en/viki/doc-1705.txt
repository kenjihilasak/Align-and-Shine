Lisbon
Lisbon is the capital of Portugal.
It's considered a global city by virtue of its considerable importance in sectors such as finance, commerce, publishing, art, international trade, education and tourism.
It is an important economic center, with a growing financial sector and one of the most important ports on the Atlantic.
In February Carnival begins, the streets of the city are filled with colorful parades, music and wild dances.
