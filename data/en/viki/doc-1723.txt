2020 is year in the 21st century.
It was a very turbulent time for many, mainly because of the outbreak of the COVID-19 virus.
the pandemic caused by this virus had led to multiple lockdowns and businesses and schools closed around the world.
the pandemic has been likened to the black death/plague pandemic in the 13th to 16th centuries.
other notable events include: Chadwick Boseman's death, black lives matter protests, Impeachment of President Donald Trump, Kobe Bryant's death, Prince Harry and Meghan Markle quit royal family, Australian bushfires, "Parasite" sweeps the Oscars, Harvey Weinstein verdict, Stock market crash 2020 and Biden becomes Democratic presidential nominee.
2020 was also the start of a new decade, coming before 2021 and after 2019.
