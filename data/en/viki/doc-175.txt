The Boy in the Striped Pyjamas
The boy in the striped pyjamas was created by John Boyne on January 5, 2006.
It has 216 pages, has been translated by Catherine Gibert.
The novel style is historical and post modern.

After a visit of Adolf Hitler, Bruno's father is promoted commandant.
