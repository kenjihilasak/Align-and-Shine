Circle
A circle is a geometrical figure where all its points are equidistant from a single point (the center).
This distance is called the radius.
A circle is empty, a filled circle is a disc.
The perimeter of a circle is given by 2πr{\displaystyle 2\pi r}, where r{\displaystyle r} is the radius of the circle.
The area or a circle is given by πr2{\displaystyle \pi r^{2}}.
