London is the capital city of the United Kingdom.
It is located in England and crossed by the Thames.
Its area is about 1580 km2, and it has more than 7,700,000 inhabitants.
The merchants of the City played an essential political role in Tudor and Stuart times.
The current mayor of London is Sadiq Khan.
He was elected in 2016.
There are 6 football clubs in London.
The most famous are Arsenal and Chelsea.
It is also in London that the Wimbledon tournament is held every year.
It is the oldest tennis tournament in the world.
It is one of the 4 Grand Slams (with Roland-Garros, the US Open and the Australian Open).
