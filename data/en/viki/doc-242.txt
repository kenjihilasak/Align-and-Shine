The Tortoise and the Hare
A hare mocks a slow-moving tortoise.
The tortoise challenges the hare to a race.
The hare agrees, then speeds ahead of the tortoise.
Halfway through the race, the hare takes a nap.
The tortoise passes him and wins the race.
"The Tortoise and the Hare" has been adapted to short animated movies including a Silly Symphony by Walt Disney Productions in 1934 and an MGM Merry Melodies in 1941.
