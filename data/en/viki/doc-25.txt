Savanna in March in Benin
The Savanna or savannah is a type of biome that exist in tropical and subtropical regions.
There are tall and closely grasses and scaterred bushes and trees.
In the dry season grasses fade.
But there is lots of grasses that can regrow from the roots in the wet season.
In Northern Australia, savanna have scaterred eucalyptus trees.
