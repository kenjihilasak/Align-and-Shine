Buenos Aires
According to the 2010 census, the city proper had 2,891,082 people.
The urban area had 13.528 million people according to a 2011 estimate.
About 14 million people take the subway of Buenos Aries every month.
Buenos Aires inhabitants are called porteño (masculine) and porteña (feminine).
