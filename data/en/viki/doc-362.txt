Caesio teres
Diver surrounded by Yellow and Blueback Fusiliers (Caesio teres, with yellow tails) and Blue-and-gold Fusiliers (Caesio caerulaurea, with dark tails).
Great Barrier Reef
Caesio teres, the yellow and blueback fusilier, is a pelagic marine tropicalfish (family Caesionidae).
Its caudal fin is forked.
Its body coloration is blue and yellow on the back.
