Grumpy Cat is a lonely cat living in a pet shop in a shopping mall.
The pet shop is going to go out of business and be shut down.
Grumpy is sad and upset because she is not getting picked to get a new home, while other animals are being adopted.
Chrystal is a lonely 12-year-old girl who comes into the pet store right before Christmas and falls in love with Grumpy Cat.
Grumpy finds out that she can communicate by talking with this girl.
Chrystal is also amazed to find out that Grumpy Cat can talk.
At first, the two do not get along with one another.
While visiting in the pet shop during the Christmas holidays, they slowly become friends.
Chrystal really likes Grumpy after they have an exciting adventure.
A dog that costs a lot of money is about to be dognapped from the pet shop.
Even though Grumpy does not like the dog, she stops the theft of the dog from happening.
Reviews for Grumpy Cat's Worst Christmas Ever have been mostly negative.
The AV Club and Entertainment Weekly both did not like the movie.
AV Club said it was "the largest turd in Lifetime's crap crown of original programming."
The Guardian and St. Louis Post Dispatch were a bit more positive in their reviews.
The St. Louis Post Dispatch said that although the film was "terrible" they still enjoyed it overall.
