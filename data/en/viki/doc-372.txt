Metal
Metals, sometimes called Heavy Metals, hard rock etc. Is a kind of rock music, performed more aggressively than the other rocks.
Metals started in the late 1960s and early 1970s, mostly in the US and UK.
Famous metal bands
Metal music got famous in the 1980s.
Some of the famous metal bands were AC/DC, Metalica, Guns and Roses, Bon Jovi etc.
