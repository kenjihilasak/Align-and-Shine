Antelope
The antelope is an herbivore mammal.
It lives in countries in the Eastern Hemisphere.
It has long, brown horns and its fur is dark brown, white, or light brown.
They eat vegetables and juicy plants.
The antelope is about 1.10 meters.
The antelope lives in India and in Africa.
They live in herds of 50 or 60 individuals.
The descend of antelope is Dik-Dik, a small antelope.
This animal is in the history of the leopard and the antelope, an African tale.
The antelope has an important part in Indian theology.
