Snow White
An illustration from a 19th-century edition of the story
Snow White is a Germanfairy tale.
It was probably told for centuries, but in 1812 the Brothers Grimm published a version they had collected in their Grimms' Fairy Tales.
Snow White is a beautiful princess.
She is cast out by her jealous stepmother, the Queen.
The princess finds a home in the forest with seven dwarfs who are very kind to her.
The Queen learns where she is living and poisons her.
Snow White does not die but sleeps.
She is awakened by a handsome prince.
They marry and live happily ever after.
