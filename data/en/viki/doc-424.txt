Steve Jobs
Steven Paul Jobs also known as Steve Jobs (February 24, 1955 - October 5, 2011) was an American business magnate and investor.
He was best known as the co-founder, chairman and CEO of Apple Inc..
He was responsible for launching the iPod, the iPhone and the iPad.
In the 1970's he built the first Apple computer, alongside with Steve Wozniak.
He was also a member of The Walt Disney Company's board of directors.
He died from a rare form of pancreatic cancer on October 5, 2011, in Palo Alto, California, United States.
His current net worth was estimated to be around $10.2 billion dollars.
