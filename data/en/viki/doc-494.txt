John Boyne
John Boyne was born in Dublin, Ireland on April 30, 1971 (44 years old).
He studied Englishliterature and writing initially at Trinity College then at the university of Norwich.
He started to write at the age of 20.
He had 70 novels and article published, among which the most famous, The Boy in the striped Pyjamas was adapted to the cinema, translated into 35 languages and sold to 15 million specimen.
He is an author of novels and his novels are aimed at every generations.
