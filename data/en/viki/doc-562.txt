Richard Nixon
Richard Nixon (January 9, 1913 - April 22, 1994) was the 37th president of the United States.
He was president from 20 January 1969 - 9 August 1974.
He was the first (and only) president to resign from office.
He resigned because his involvement in the Watergate Scandal was revealed.
He died on 22 April 1994 (aged 81) from a stroke.
