Charles Dickens, about 1868
Charles John Huffam Dickens was a British writer.
He was born on 7 February 1812 in Landport, Portsmouth, Hampshire, England.
He married Catherine Thomson Hogarth.
The couple had ten children.
He died on 9 June 1870 (aged 58) at Gad's Hill Place, Higham, Kent, England.
He was buried in Poets' Corner, Westminster Abbey.
