Autumn
September, October, and November are generally considered the months of autumn in the Northern Hemisphere.
However, it officially begins on the autumnal equinox (mid September) and ends on the winter solstice (mid December).
March, April, and May are generally considered the months of autumn in the Southern Hemisphere.
However, it officially begins on the autumnal equinox (mid March) and ends on the winter solstice (mid June).
