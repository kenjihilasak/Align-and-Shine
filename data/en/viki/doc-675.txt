Super Mario 3D World
Super Mario 3D world is a game for the Wii U published by Nintendo.
The Sprixie princesses are trapped in jars and it’s up to Mario Princess Peach or blue toad to save them from bowser.
You can collect green stars which are used to unlock locations.
Another game called super Mario 3d world + Bowser fury has the addition of bowser fury and its for the Nintendo Switch.
