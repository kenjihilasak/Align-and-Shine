Amsterdamsche Football Club "Ajax", or AFC Ajax or just Ajax, is a Dutch professional football club from Amsterdam.
Since 1956, Ajax plays in the highest Dutch division: the Eredivisie.
Ajax has also become 33 times Dutch champion and has won the Champions League 4 times.
Ajax could also receive the Badge of Honor because they won the Champions League 3 times later (then the Europacup I).
The home of Ajax is the Amsterdam ArenA. Ajax also has a women's team.
