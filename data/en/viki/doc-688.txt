Water
Water is a liquid.
It is made from two molecules of hydrogen, and one molecule of oxygen.
The chemical formula for water is written as H2O. Thus, water is a compound made up of oxygen and hydrogen.
Water is clear, and usually in its purest form has no smell.
Water will boil at 212 degrees Fahrenheit
[F] .
Water will freeze 32 degrees F (0 degrees C).
Water can be seen in oceans, rivers, and lakes.
