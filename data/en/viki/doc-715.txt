Scottish National Party
The Scottish National Party (SNP) is a political party in the United Kingdom, which is active in Scotland.
The SNP supports and campaigns for Scottish independence in the European Union.
It's the third largest party in the UK and the largest party in Scotland.
The SNP is led by Nicola Sturgeon, who is the First Minister of Scotland.
The SNP is nationalistic and wants Scotland to become its own country.
The party is pro-European.
The party wants a second Scottisch independence referendum after the Brexit.
If the Scots vote in favor of independence, the SNP wants Scotland to become a EU member state.
