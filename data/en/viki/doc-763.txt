Satoru Iwata
Satoru Iwata at GDC 2011
Satoru Iwata (岩田 聡 Iwata Satoru; December 6, 1959, Sapporo, Japan - July 12, 2015, Kyoto, Japan) was the fourth CEO of Nintendo.
He first worked at HAL Laboratory as a freelance programmer.
He went full-time after completing college.
He became president of Nintendo after Hiroshi Yamauchi retired in 2002.
He became Nintendo of America's CEO in 2013.
Iwata died of a bile duct tumor at age 55.
