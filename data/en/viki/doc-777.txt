Philippe Apeloig is a graphic designer who lives in France.
He was born in November 1962.
1997, "Poster in the context of French culture", Galerie GGG, Tokyo
1999, "The Lingering Memory", Cooper Union School of Art, Houghton Gallery, NY
2008, "Vivo in Typo", Espace Topographique de l'art, Paris
2011, "Bewegte Schrift", exposition de groupe, Museum für Gestaltung, Zürich
Gold Award, Tokyo Type Director Club (1995),
Gold Award, Hong Kong International Poster Triennial (2007)
Overall Winner, International Society of Typographic Designers of London (2009)
