Michelangelo
Chalk portrait of Michelangelo by Daniele da Volterra
Michelangelo was a sculptor, an architect, a painter, an engineer, and poet of the Italian Renaissance.
Many consider him the greatest artist of all time.
His full name is Michelangelo di Lodovico Buonarroti Simoni.
He was born on 6 March 1475 near Florence, Italy, and died on 18 February 1564 in Rome.
