Belo Horizonte
Belo Horizonte is a city in Minas Gerais, Brazil.
It is located in the southeast part of the country.
In 2013, the city proper had 2,479,165 people; and in 2010, the urban area had 5,414,701 people.
The urban area of Belo Horizonte is the third largest in Brazil, after São Paulo and Rio de Janeiro.
