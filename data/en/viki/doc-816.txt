Dietrich started her theatrical career in 1921.
She made her first movie for Germany in 1923.
In 1930, the movie The Blue Angel made her famous.
In 1939, she received American nationality to denounce Nazism.
During World War II, she helped American soldiers by entertaining them.
In 1964, she became sick and said she would continue making movies only for money.
In 1975, she broke her neck, and quit the movies.
She died in 1992, at 91.
