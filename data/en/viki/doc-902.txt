Archaeopteryx
The Archaeopteryx is a bird like dinosaur from the late Jurassic period.
It sometimes joins flocks with other bird like dinosaurs.
When the Archaeopteryx was discovered scientist believed it was the proof of evolution.
The Archaeopteryx live around 150 million years ago during the late jurassic period
The Archaeopteryx was the size of a common raven (Crow).
It has feathers that were similar to common birds now.
The bird like dinosaur had hallow bones similar to birds
