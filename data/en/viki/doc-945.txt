Angry Birds is a 2009 Rovio Entertainment logic video game made for iOS, Maemo, Android, Symbian, Windows Phone and BlackBerry 10 systems.
He became very famous which led to the creation of the Angry Birds franchise.
In Angry Birds games, the player must use his fingers on the screen to kill the pigs using a slingshot.
