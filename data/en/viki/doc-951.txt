Winnipeg
Winnipeg is a city in southern Canada, located where the Red River and Assiniboine River meet.
It is the capital of the province of Manitoba, and Manitoba's largest city.
In 2016, it had 705,244 people living in it.
It is also sometimes called "Winterpeg," because it is cold in the winter.
It was created so that people could trade with each other more easily.
It became a city in 1873.
