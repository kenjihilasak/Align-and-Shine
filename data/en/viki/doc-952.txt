Cannes
Cannes' port and Croisette.
Cannes is a touristic city located in south of France, in the department of Alpes-Maritimes.
This city has 73,600 inhabitants (2012) and an area of 19.62 km².
The city is known worldwide for its annual summer event, the Cannes Film Festival, where famous actors and producers come to present and promote their films to the public.
The Promenade de la Croisette.
Cannes has a major economic environment based on tourism and business.
It has developed high technology activities with Sophia Antipolis' technopolis.
