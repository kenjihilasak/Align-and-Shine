Alabama
Alabama is a state in the southeastern United States.
As of 2015, it had 4,858,979 people.
Alabama's nickname is the Yellowhammer State, because the state bird is called a Yellowhammer.
It is also sometimes called the "Heart of Dixie", and "The Cotton State".
Native Americans (the first American people) lived in Alabama long before Europeans moved there.
They were very diverse with varying cultures.
