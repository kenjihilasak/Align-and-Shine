The Free Danes () was a Danish resistance newspaper published in Copenhagen about monthly from December 1941 to 24 May 1945.
It was the first Danish non-communist resistance newspaper and the first to bring photographs and also one of the largest with final issues reaching a circulation of 20,000.
Especially notable was the June 1944 Invasion Issue titled 'The Free Danes Welcome our Allied Friends' with a four colored front page photo of one US and one British rifleman each in front of their national flags.
In popular culture
The newspaper appears in Lois Lowry's historical novel Number the Stars.
