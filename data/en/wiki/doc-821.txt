Elizabeth Anne Greene is an American actress best known for her title role as Dawn Harper in the Nickelodeon sitcom Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn from 2014 to 2018.
She has been starring as Sophie Dixon in the ABC family drama A Million Little Things since 2018.
Life and career
Greene was born in Dallas, Texas.
She made her television debut in the Nickelodeon sitcom Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn in the title role of Dawn Harper, the only girl of quadruplets.
The show ran from September 13, 2014 to August 4, 2018.
In 2018, she was cast as Sophie Dixon in the ABC drama A Million Little Things.
